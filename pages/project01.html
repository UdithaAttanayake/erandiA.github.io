Overview

Provided a SaaS product for children's charities. Guided by a commitment to listening, acting, and advocating for the safety of at-risk children, developed family support programs and services to empower children to realize their full potential. This dashboard is designed for a nonprofit organization.It's used to visualize their data by connecting to their existing operational systems , databases and visualizing millions of data points crucial to the operations of organizations.

Challenge

Addressing the challenge involves extracting and categorizing incidents faced by children, ensuring key details like dates are captured. Subsequently, tasks and solutions are assigned based on the categorization to address each incident appropriately.

Given their sole reliance on a web application, the task at hand involves constructing a B2C Dashboard tailored to monitor customer behavior comprehensively. This entails tracking feature usage , event occurrence, and user activity meticulously. Additionally, employing funnel analysis facilitates the breakdown of the onboarding process to pinpoint any friction points. Utilizing Flow analytics further aids in evaluating the efficacy of onboarding strategies, thereby identifying key drivers of success.

The Problem

  • Current monitoring, reporting, and intervention strategies are inadequate in effectively addressing child protection concerns.
  • Child protection agencies face challenges in gathering, analyzing, and acting upon data related to child abuse cases.
  • The prevalence of child labor, trafficking, and abuse is exacerbated by socio-economic factors such as poverty and drug abuse.
  • Inadequate monitoring and reporting mechanisms lead to delayed identification and intervention in child abuse cases, resulting in many instances going unreported and unaddressed.
  • This perpetuates the cycle of abuse and undermines the overall well-being and development of affected children, leading to long-term psychological and physical health issues.
  • Inefficiencies in the current system hinder child protection agencies' ability to plan and allocate resources effectively, limiting their capacity to provide comprehensive support to survivors of violence.

Research

#User Interviews

The insights from user interviews provide valuable guidance for the design and implementation , aiming to address user preferences and pain points to enhance the overall onboarding experience for potential customers.

01
Users expressed a preference for interactive onboarding experiences that are accessible to non-technical individuals. They emphasized the importance of these experiences in helping them understand the product’s features and functionalities.
02
Users voiced concerns about the use of scroll bars and drop-down menus, indicating that they perceived them as obstacles to accessing data directly. They found these elements to be time-consuming and expressed a desire for a more immediate and hassle-free experience.
03
Users highlighted the need to extract incident types, including incidents, sub-incidents, liked incidents, and related incidents, simultaneously. They emphasized the importance of being able to view these various types within the context of their timeline.

I've gathered insights from their website and social media platforms.By addressing these findings and implementing strategic changes, Barnardo's we were able to enhance the user experience, reduce drop-off points, and ultimately improve the acquisition process for potential customers.
